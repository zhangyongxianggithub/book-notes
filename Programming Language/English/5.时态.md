时态: 主要指的是动词，动词随着时间来改变形态。
# 现在(简单)式
- be动词(状态，存在): am/are/is
- 一般动词(动作)，主词为第三人称单数动词需要加s

时间副词: now,every+时间。
- 表示现在的状态或者动作时使用现在式。例句:
  - There are many visitors in the zoo
  - Here comes the bus
- 表示现在习惯性的动作。例句:
  - David often sleeps during class
  - My parents take exercise(play sports) in the park every morning
- 表示不变的事实与真理
  - The earth moves around the sun
# 过去(简单)式
- be动词: was/were
- 一般动词: 过去式动词(不分人称)，规则变化是词尾+ed或者ied，也有不规则变化

时间副词: 
- yesterday(morning、afternoon、evening)
- the day before yesterday
- last+时间表示上一个，比如last week等
- 时间+ago，比如two hours ago、five days ago
- before(以前)
- then，at that time

使用场景
- 表示过去的动作或者状态
  - I bought this yesterday
  - There was an old temple over there
- 表示过去习惯性的动作(used to 原形动词，表示过去习惯如何如何)
  - My father used to smoke， but now he dosen't
# 现在进行式
be动词+Ving，be动词表示现在的含义，Ving表示进行，表示正在什么什么的，Ving表示现在分词。
- 规则形式是原形动词+ing
- 不规则的形式有结尾为e去e加ing，比如coming
- 原形动词为子音+短母音+子音，重复字尾+ing，比如putting、cutting、swimming
  - We are eating breakfast now
  - 介系词后面出现动词要加ing，We ate breakfast before(介系词) going to school
  - We eat breakfast every morning

使用场景:
- 表示现在正在进行中的动作
  - John is watching the baseball game on TV now
- 表示重复发生的动作，常伴随always、all the time、again and again
  - He is always complaining
  - The car is breaking down all the time
- 表示最近的未来即将发生的动作，常用于某些动词如come、go、start、leave、arrive(来去动词)
  - I'm leaving for Kenting tomorrow
  - My boyfriend is coming to see me this afternoon
- 不能用于进行式的动词
  - 表示感官的动词，see、hear、smell
  - 表示情感的动词，love、like、hate
  - 其他如have、know等
# 过去进行式
动词形式: was/were +Ving
使用场景:
- 表示过去某一个时间点正在进行的动作
  - We were playing chess at eight yesterday evening
  - Lily was taking a bath when the doorbell rang
- 表示过去时间某一期限中反复性的动作
  - Whenever I visited him, he was watching TV
  - In those days, we were getting up at six o'clock
# 未来式
用来表示发生于未来的动作或者状态，be going to 原形动词。be going to 表示用于实现性非常高的事或者事先计划好的未来
时间副词
- tomorrow(morning\afternoon\evening)
- the day after tomorrow
- next 时间(下一个)，next week、next year
- in 时间(在什么当中)，in a few days/in a week，表示多久之后

例句
- I'm going to visit my uncle tomorrow
- I have to buy the ladder because I am going to paint the house
- I don't feel good, I'm afraid I'm going to be sick
- Are they going to have a party on Christmas Eve

未来式的助动词will，will+原形动词，will表示将要
- We will leave junior high school
- We are going to leave junior high school
- We are leaving junior high school
- I will be fifteen years old next year
- I can't move this large box, I'll do it for you
- I will not = I'll not = I won't 

will you ~~ 表示请求或者邀约
Will you ～～? 可以有以下回答
- Sure/OK/All right
- Yes please/thank you
- No thank you

例句
- Will you look after the baby for me?Sure
